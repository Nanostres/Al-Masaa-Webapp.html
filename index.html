Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services does AL MASAA AL BAYDHA provide?

We provide a comprehensive range of PRO services including trade license services, business setup, visa and immigration services, document attestation, Emirates ID services, and more.

How long does it take to process a residence visa?

The residence visa process typically takes 2-3 weeks from start to finish, including entry permit approval, medical testing, Emirates ID registration, and visa stamping.

Can foreigners own 100% of a business in the UAE?

Yes, foreigners can now own 100% of businesses in the UAE mainland in most business activities, following recent changes to the Commercial Companies Law.

What documents are required for trade license renewal?

For trade license renewal, you typically need the original trade license, tenancy contract, passport copies of all partners/owners, Emirates ID copies, and other documents specific to your business type.
